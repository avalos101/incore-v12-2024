POS Email Receipt

When a customer places an order from your POS shop then sometimes he wishes that instead of getting a hard copy of the receipt he should get the same on his email address. So considering this we have implemented our module inCore POS Email Receipt using which the seller can send an e-receipt to the customer instead of printing it. Also if a customer wishes to receive the receipt on a different email address, then, the seller can specify a different email address while the customer's primary email address still remains the same.

Features:

  • Seller can send E-Receipts to his customers from POS.
  • A customer sometimes may wish to receive the receipt on a different email address. In this case, seller can specify a different email while the customer's primary email still remains the same.
  • Seller also has an option to update the customer's primary email address while sending an E-Receipt.
