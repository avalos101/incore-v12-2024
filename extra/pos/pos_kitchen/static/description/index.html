POS Kitchen

Easy way to organise your kitchen orders

Features
  • Easily manage our kitchen orders coming from different POS Session.
  • User friendly kitchen interface.
  • We can enter extra note in orderline.
  • we can assign priority to orders.
  • You can print multiple Receipts of Kitchen Order in Kitchen View.
  • Receipt is printed by Thermal Printer.
